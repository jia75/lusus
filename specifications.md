## Chessboard format
120-element array:  
[21] is a1  
[28] is h1  
[91] is a8  
[98] is h8  

## Piece format
0: no piece  
1 through 6: White pieces, King to Pawn (K Q R B N P)  
7 through 12: Black pieces, King to Pawn (k q r b n p)  
13: border square  

## Move format
4 digit number:  
ABCD  
AB: starting square  
CD: destination square
